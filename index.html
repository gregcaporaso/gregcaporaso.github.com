We are a bioinformatics software engineering lab interested in applications of microbiome science in cancer prevention, cancer treatment, and environmental sustainability.

We additionally lead activities focused on bioinformatics education and training, and increasing interest in and awareness of careers in scientific computing in pre-college students.

Our software and educational content are free and open source, and we're committed to increasing accessibility and building community around reproducible scientific computing and microbiome science.
